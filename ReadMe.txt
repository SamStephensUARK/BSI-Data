Repository of data associated with a manuscript.

Geometry Files contain the CAD files used for in-vitro experiments and domains of the fluid-structure interaction simulations.

Direct Pressure Measurements contain the raw LabView measurement data for the direct pressure measurements. The raw data in in volts and need to be multiplied by 100 to obtain pressure in mmHg. The columns labeled Millar 1 and Millar 2 correspond to the inlet and outlet, respectively.
